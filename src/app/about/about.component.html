Our Team

Sayyed Nisar Ahmad

Founder

Belongs to the city of Firozabad, which is the center of India's glassmaking industry and is known for the glassware produced. Migrated to Mumbai in 1975 and started the small business of Crockery Decorations, today the company is specialized in printing on glass containers working as suppliers of printed glass bottles to Lakme.

Sayed Farhat Ali

Project Lead

Inherited the tradition of glass printing from his father Sayed Nisar, today Farhat Ali expanded the business in the field of frosting, printing and coating. He is a promoter of automation and build the business around it taking it to the next level. Most of the machines are designed in India for India by Indian genius.
